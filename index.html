UNBOX the idea

It all started in 2018, says the Unbox Co. founder, "this history began as we tried to build a coworking space and needed a good brand. We began construction and, for countless reasons, it had to stop, we, however, had already created the brand that you know today... a friend who worked as a marketeer helped me to put on paper the idea of "unboxing ideas".


The UNBOX concept was clearly NOT about software development

The brand was initially created to be a coworking space, but it also went out to be the abstract idea of a brand for some other genious business ideas:

  1. a coworking space (as already especified)
  2. a maritime transportation company
  3. a clothing brand, yes, you may believe it.

As you can see, aside from what some would suppose, the UNBOX concept wasn't dreamt or designed because the founder started a software company, it was a dream of many years before, the dream of a company that wouldn't do things just like all the others, UNBOX is the dream of creating something through critical and inspired thinking.
